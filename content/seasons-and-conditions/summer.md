title: Summer
tgs: 2026-07-23
---
Summer is a fun time to fish but it can also be the hottest time to do it. Finding a shaded area, having an umbrella, or wearing hats is ideal for keeping the Texas sunshine from your face. For freshwater fishing, largemouth bass, crappie, and most freshwater fish enjoy being near deep structures or under a shaded area to cool off, and they will do this especially in the afternoons. 

When saltwater fishing, you can expect to see a good number of trout and redfish, who are both known to be aggressive feeders in the summer.
