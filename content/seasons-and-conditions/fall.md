---
title: Fall
tgs: 2026-07-23
---
Due to the weather cooling in the fall, fish activity increases, causing them to go after bait exceptionally more than in the summer time. For anglers that have a hard time dealing with the heat of the summer, the fall is a good time for you. 

Fall is peak bite time for sought after freshwater fish like largemouth bass and catfish. For saltwater, the fish in general are going to be in a biting frenzy, particularly the redfish, some refer to October as the "Bull Red Run" due to the increased visibility of redfish. 

>Fish in all waters tend to go on a feeding frenzy in the fall.
