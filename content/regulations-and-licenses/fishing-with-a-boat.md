### Important Boat Tips

- **Check the weather before departing your boat.**
- Secure your gear properly and avoid overloading your boat.
- Remain seated as much possible to avoid falling overboard.
- Never anchor from the back of the boat; anchor from the front.
- Clean and drain water from your boat after leaving or going into freshwater. Keep the boat dry to prevent invasive species from spreading

## Safety Rules

- Texas law states that a type I, II, or III wearable-type life jacket must be available for each person on board and it must be US Coast Guard approved. A type IV, Throwable-type flotation devices must be on a boat that is 16 feet or more in length. 
- Texas law prohibits intoxicated individuals with a .08% BAC from operating a boat.
- Children under 13 must wear a life jacket while a boat under 26 feet in length is moving.
- Unless there is a legally qualified operator older than 18, anyone born on or after September 1, 1993 must complete an approved education course when operating a boat of more than 15 horse power.
